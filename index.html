Welcome to Top Chef

Judging

How it works.

Each chef will be asked to present 3 stunning dishes of their choice.

You, as our honorary guests, will be asked to judge each chefs' execution, presentation and taste. A maximum of 10 points per category may be awarded with a combined score of 30 points. Their fault rests on your taste buds.

Good luck, and may the odds forever be in your flavor.
